### Brakes Squeaking
Squeaky brakes can be annoying and might indicate a potential safety issue. This noise could be caused by worn brake pads or other underlying problems. How can this problem be fixed?
1. Inspect the brake pads and replace if worn.
2. Check the rotors for damage and resurface if needed.
3. Lubricate the brake components.
4. Ensure the brake calipers are functioning correctly.
5. Remove any debris from the braking system.
